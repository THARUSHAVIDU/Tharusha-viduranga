About IoT

Introduction to IoT

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to the network of physical objects embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity to exchange data with other devices and systems over the Internet.

Key Features of IoT

IoT devices can collect real-time data, communicate with each other, automate processes, and improve efficiency. Applications range from smart homes, industrial automation, healthcare, to agriculture.

Importance of IoT

IoT allows businesses and individuals to monitor systems remotely, reduce manual intervention, save resources, and enable intelligent decision-making based on real-time insights.

Job Opportunities in IoT

IoT Developer

IoT developers design and develop smart devices, embedded software, and cloud integrations to connect physical devices with the internet.

IoT Solutions Architect

Solutions architects plan, design, and implement IoT ecosystems for businesses, including network connectivity, data flow, and analytics.

IoT Data Analyst / Engineer

Professionals work with IoT data collected from sensors to analyze, optimize processes, and provide actionable insights.

IoT Security Specialist

Security experts ensure that IoT systems are safe from cyber threats, protecting data and connected devices from vulnerabilities.
